Our Features ▼

We built our service around your convenience. Our key features include:

  • Card Payment on Arrival: No need to pay in advance. Just book online and pay with your card at pickup.
  • No Deposit Required: Enjoy your trip without a large deposit hold on your card.
  • Airport Pickup & Dropoff: We meet you at the airport arrivals terminal, saving you time.
  • Unlimited Kilometers: Drive as much as you want without worrying about extra fees.
  • 24/7 Roadside Support: Peace of mind knowing we are just a phone call away, any time of day.
Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) ▼

Q: What do I need to rent a car?

A: You need a valid driver's license (held for at least 1 year), a valid ID or passport, and to be at least 18 years old.

Q: Is a deposit required?

A: No, we offer rentals with no deposit required.

Q: How do I pay?

A: We only accept card payment on arrival. You do not need a card to book, but you must have a valid card for payment when you pick up the car.

How It Works (Tutorial) ▼

Renting a car with us is simple:

  • 1. Browse Our Fleet: Choose the car that best suits your needs from our inventory.
  • 2. Book Online: Click 'Book Now', select your pickup and return dates, and fill in your details. No card is needed to book.
  • 3. Pick Up Your Car: Meet us at the agreed location (like the airport). We will finalize the paperwork and take the payment.
  • 4. Pay on Arrival: Pay securely with your card when you pick up the car. We do not accept cash.
  • 5. Return the Car: Drop off the car at the agreed time and location. It's that easy!
Terms & Conditions ▼

By booking a car with UINRENT, you agree to our full terms and conditions. Key terms include: Renter must be 18+ with a valid license. The vehicle must be returned in the same condition as it was received. Unlimited mileage is included. All rentals are subject to availability. Full terms will be provided with your rental agreement.

Privacy Policy ▼

We respect your privacy. We only collect the personal information necessary to process your booking (name, email, phone number). This information is stored securely in our Firebase database and is never shared with third parties. We use EmailJS to send booking confirmations. We do not store any payment card information.
